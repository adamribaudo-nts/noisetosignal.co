### The Problem: AI is Here to Help, but the Hard Parts Haven't Changed
Executive stakeholders know that putting an LLM on top of raw, messy data leads to hallucinations and incorrect metrics. To compensate, organizations have spent years locking their business logic inside proprietary Business Intelligence (BI) tools. 

But relying on the BI layer limits your AI potential:
* **The BI Bottleneck:** Getting a new dimensional breakdown requires a drawn-out process of back-and-forth design and ticketing with data analysts.
* **Walled Gardens:** If your data modeling and metric definitions live entirely inside a BI tool, your LLMs can't natively access them.
* **Rigid Consumption:** Stakeholders don't usually need a complex dashboard design; they just need the answer to a business question. 

### The Solution: A Governed Semantic Layer for AI
Instead of modeling data inside the BI layer, **Noise to Signal** moves your business logic upstream into a universal **Semantic Layer**. 

By decoupling metrics from dashboards, we create a structured, well-governed environment where LLMs can safely and accurately query your data warehouse. We leverage industry-leading tools like **dbt** and **CredibleData** to ensure your AI gives the right answer, every time, using exact organizational definitions.

### What This Unlocks for Your Organization

### Our Process: From Warehouse to Conversational AI
We offer a streamlined, end-to-end service to get your organization chatting with its data.

#### 1. Assess & Align
We start by evaluating your current data warehouse architecture and identifying a high-value, high-impact domain to serve as the pilot. We define the core metrics, dimensions, and business logic required.

#### 2. Build the Semantic Layer
We extract business logic from your BI tools and build a robust semantic layer (using **dbt** or **CredibleData**). This layer defines your metrics as code, creating a single, governed source of truth that is machine-readable.

#### 3. Hook in the LLMs
We connect the newly built semantic layer to an LLM interface. Instead of the LLM trying to write complex SQL against raw tables, it uses the semantic layer's API to pull governed metrics instantly and accurately. 

#### 4. Evaluate & Iterate
We rigorously test the conversational output for accuracy and usability with your stakeholders. Once validated, we iterate to bring in more data domains, scaling your AI capabilities securely.
